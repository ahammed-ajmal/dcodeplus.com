COURSE OFFERED

Preparatory (Zelta)

The preparatory level Zelta at Dcode+ aims to provide a strong foundation in English language skills for students who are new to the language. This course is specifically designed to cater to beginners who have little or no prior experience in English. The course offers comprehensive training in basic reading, writing, listening and speaking skills in English. Through a combination of interactive classroom sessions and practical exercises, students will learn how to read simple texts, write basic sentences, understand spoken English and communicate effectively in day-to-day situations. The course curriculum has been carefully crafted by experienced faculty members at Dcode+ to ensure that students progress at a comfortable pace and gain confidence in their ability to use the language. Personal attention is given to each student to ensure that they are able to grasp the concepts taught in class. At Dcode+, we believe that learning a new language should be an affordable and accessible experience for everyone. Our preparatory level Zelta course is reasonably priced to ensure that students from all walks of life can avail of high-quality English language training. Join Dcode+ today and take the first step towards improving your English language skills!

Intermediate (Felta)

Felta at Dcode+ is an intermediate-level course designed for individuals who have already acquired basic knowledge of English and are looking to advance their skills. This course is suitable for those who have completed the Dcode+ preparatory level or have minimum proficiency in English. The Felta course focuses on enhancing reading, writing, listening, and speaking skills to a higher level of proficiency. It covers advanced grammar, vocabulary, and pronunciation, allowing students to communicate effectively in various settings. The course is taught by experienced and qualified instructors who use a communicative and interactive approach to teaching. Students are encouraged to participate in group discussions, debates, presentations, and other collaborative activities, which help improve their confidence and fluency. At the beginning of the course, a level-checking test is conducted to assess the students' proficiency level and identify any areas that need improvement. This enables the instructors to tailor the course content to meet the specific needs of each student and provide personalized attention to help them progress faster. Overall, Felta is an excellent course for individuals who want to take their English language skills to the next level and succeed in their personal and professional lives.

Advanced (Apex)

Dcode+ Apex is designed for students who want to take their English proficiency to the next level. This course is ideal for those who have already mastered the basics of the English language and are looking to refine their speaking style, improve their vocabulary, and gain confidence in their communication skills. Through this course, you will learn to speak English with a natural fluency and a refined accent. Our experienced instructors will guide you through a range of speaking exercises, including role-playing, public speaking, and debates, to help you build your speaking confidence. In addition to speaking skills, Dcode+ Apex also focuses on enhancing your listening, reading, and writing skills. You will work on developing your vocabulary and grammar, and learn to use advanced language structures in your writing and speaking. At Dcode+, we understand that each student has their own unique learning needs. That's why our instructors work closely with each student to develop a personalized learning plan that matches their learning style and goals. Our aim is to help you achieve high competency and confidence in the English language. Enroll in Dcode+ Apex today and take the next step in your English language journey!

Decodeplus IELTS and TOEFL

Decodeplus School of Languages offers comprehensive programs for both the IELTS (International English Language Testing System) and TOEFL (Test of English as a Foreign Language) examinations. These programs are designed to equip individuals with the necessary skills and strategies to excel in these tests. With experienced instructors, interactive lessons, and practice materials, students develop their listening, reading, writing, and speaking abilities in English. The curriculum focuses on enhancing vocabulary, grammar, and pronunciation, while also providing guidance on test-taking techniques specific to each exam. Whether preparing for academic pursuits or professional aspirations, Decodeplus School of Languages ensures students are well-prepared to achieve their desired scores and succeed in English-speaking environments.

Dcodeplus Arabic English Translation

Dcodeplus Arabic English Translation course is a specialized program designed for individuals seeking certification and employment opportunities in Gulf or multinational companies. This comprehensive course equips students with the skills and knowledge necessary for accurate and professional translation between Arabic and English languages. With a focus on industry-specific terminology and cultural nuances, students develop proficiency in both written and verbal translation. The curriculum covers various domains such as business, legal, medical, and technical fields, ensuring graduates are well-prepared for the demands of the job market. With experienced instructors and practical exercises, Dcodeplus Arabic English Translation course provides students with the expertise and confidence needed to pursue successful careers as professional translators in the Gulf or multinational companies.
